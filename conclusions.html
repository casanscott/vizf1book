Visualizing Formula 1 Data in R

Chapter 9 Conclusions

I sincerely hope this book extends your data visualization capabilities. Or, at the very least, I hope reading through this book was not a complete waste of your time! I owe so much of my career enjoyment to R and ggplot2, and I wanted to pay it forward, so to speak. I fully understand that the targeted audience for a book like this is quite small! Nonetheless, I welcome feedback, comments, and questions about this book or otherwise. Feel free to email me (speedandpowerlab@gmail.com) or find me on Linkedin.
